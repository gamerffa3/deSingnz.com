About Us

de-SIGNz Interiors & Builders is a premier interior design and construction company delivering high-quality, contemporary solutions since 2003. We have successfully executed over 1200+ projects ranging from luxury residences to corporate offices, retail spaces, and hospitality interiors.

Our expertise covers false ceilings, grid ceilings, wall partitions, modular kitchens, wardrobes, glass partitions, ACP cladding, painting & polishing, EPS Rapid Wall Panels, and AAC Panel installations. Every project reflects superior craftsmanship, innovation, and adherence to timelines.

We believe in transparent pricing, detailed project planning, and complete customer satisfaction. Our design team works closely with clients to translate visions into functional, elegant spaces that stand the test of time.
